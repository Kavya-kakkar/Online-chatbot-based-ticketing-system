Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. This page explains how cancellations work for bookings made with Ciphers Inc.

General Rules

  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of booking are fully refundable.
  • Cancellations made up to 7 days before the event/service receive a 50% refund.
  • No refunds will be issued for cancellations made less than 48 hours before the event/service.

How to Cancel

To cancel your booking, please visit your account dashboard and select the cancellation option, or contact our support team.

Important Notes

  • Refunds may take 5-7 business days to process.
  • In case of technical issues or event cancellation from our side, you will receive a full refund.
  • For more details, please check our Terms & Conditions.
