Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we protect your data at Ciphers Museum.

1. Introduction

At Ciphers Museum, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and secure your information when you interact with our chatbot or website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you book tickets or contact support. Non-personal data, like browser type and usage analytics, may also be collected to enhance user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to process bookings, respond to queries, and improve our chatbot experience. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Secure payment gateways and encrypted connections are used for transactions.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences and improve functionality. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (such as payment processors or analytics platforms) that comply with data protection regulations. These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. For assistance, contact us at support@ciphersmuseum.com.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any concerns about our privacy practices, please reach out to us via email at support@ciphersmuseum.com.
